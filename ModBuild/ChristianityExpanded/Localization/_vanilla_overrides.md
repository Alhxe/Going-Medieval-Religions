# Vanilla string overrides

This mod renames some vanilla concepts to fit a Christianity theme. Adding the same key in `en.json` / `es.json` overrides the vanilla value.

| Vanilla key | Vanilla EN | Our EN | Our ES |
|---|---|---|---|
| `religion_christian_name` | Restitutionist | Christianity | Cristianismo |
| `religion_christian_description` | (vanilla) | (rewrite) | (reescritura) |
| `room_type_chapel_christian` | Restitutionist Chapel | Chapel | Capilla |
| `room_type_advanced_chapel_christian` | Restitutionist Cathedral | Cathedral | Catedral |
| `resource_name_restitutionist_relic` | Restitutionist Relic | Holy Relic | Reliquia Sagrada |

⚠️ Verify each vanilla key still exists when bumping `gameVersion`.
